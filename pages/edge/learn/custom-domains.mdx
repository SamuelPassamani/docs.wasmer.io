# Using custom domains with Wasmer Edge

You can point any custom subdomains or domains to your application by **updating the DNS records** of your domain.

Please go to your app's dashboard settings and click on the `Domains` tab.

You will see a list of domains that are already configured for your app.


<ImageLoader img={overview} alt="App Domains Overview" />

## Adding a new domain

To add a new domain, you can click on the `Add Domain` button and follow the instructions.
